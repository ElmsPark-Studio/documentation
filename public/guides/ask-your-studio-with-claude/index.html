Talk to your studio with your own Claude subscription

Ask your PageMotor site about its business in plain English, on your own Claude Pro or Max plan. No API key, no extra cost, nothing new to install.

⏱ 5 minutes to connect 💳 Uses your Claude Pro / Max plan 🔒 Read-only 🧩 Works in claude.ai, Desktop, Code June 2026

Your PageMotor site can answer questions about your business in plain English, using your own Claude subscription. It works because your site already speaks Claude’s connector language. You point Claude at your site once, sign in as the studio admin, and then you just ask.

What you need

  • A Claude Pro or Max plan (Team and Enterprise work too).
  • Your PageMotor studio site (running the EP studio suite).
  • Your PageMotor admin login for that site.

Connect it (claude.ai, in a browser)

  1. Open claude.ai and go to Settings → Connectors.
  2. Choose Add custom connector.
  3. Give it a name like My Studio, and for the URL enter your site’s connector address: https://YOUR-SITE/mcp/. For the demo site that is https://sundial.solasyogastudio.com/mcp/.
  4. Save. Claude sends you to a sign-in page. Log in with your PageMotor admin account and approve access. That is what lets Claude read your studio data on your behalf.
  5. Start a new chat, turn the My Studio connector on for that chat, and ask away.

It is the same in Claude Desktop (Settings → Connectors → add custom connector) and in Claude Code (add it as an MCP server). Use whichever Claude you already use.

What you can ask

Anything the dashboard knows, in your own words:

  • “How is the business doing this month?”
  • “Who is most at risk of leaving, and why?”
  • “Which classes next week are nearly full or nearly empty?”
  • “Who has not signed a waiver before their next class?”
  • “Whose class-pass credits are about to expire?”
  • “What does payroll owe instructors, and who is teaching today?”

Claude pulls the live figures and answers in seconds.

It is read-only. Claude can see everything and tell you about it, but it cannot make a booking, grant a pass, run payroll or change a setting. It will point you to the right screen in your dashboard to do that yourself.

Good to know

  • It is your subscription doing the work. No API key, no per-question charge beyond your normal Claude plan.
  • It is your data, your account. You sign in as your own admin, so Claude only sees what you would see.
  • You stay in control. Disconnect any time from your site’s Settings → Connected Apps, or from Claude’s own connector settings.

Prefer it built into the Admin?

There is also an “Ask” box that lives right inside your PageMotor Admin, so you never leave the dashboard. That version uses a small Anthropic API key (a few pennies of usage a day for typical questions) instead of your subscription. Same questions, same answers, just baked into the admin screen rather than your Claude app. Use whichever suits you.
