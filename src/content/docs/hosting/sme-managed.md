One site, doing real work, and nobody on your team wants to become a sysadmin. We run the VPS, you run the business.

## Who this is for

- You have one important PageMotor site: consultancy, service business, practice, small shop.
- The site does real work. Booking, ecommerce, contact forms, newsletter, live content.
- Downtime is not academic. Every hour the site is off is money that does not land.

## What you get

- **Right-sized Vultr VPS.** Dedicated box scoped to your traffic profile. Typical SME profile is a 2 vCPU, 4 GB, 100 GB plan.
- **Nightly off-site backups.** Seven-day rolling retention as standard, longer on request.
- **Best-effort 99.9 per cent uptime.** Monitored every 60 seconds, we are paged when the site stops responding. Not an SLA-backed guarantee; formal SLAs are available on request at higher tiers.
- **Core and plugin maintenance.** PageMotor and EP Suite updates tested and applied in a scheduled monthly window.
- **Security patching.** OS patches, SSH hardening, Fail2ban, firewall locked down to 22, 80 and 443.
- **SMTP wired up.** Contact form notifications, password resets and newsletter dispatch via SendGrid.
- **Support.** Same-day email support during UK working hours.

## What you bring

- A domain, registered at any registrar.
- Your content: text, images, and any existing site you want migrated across.
- Your PageMotor licence if you use paid EP Suite plugins.

## How it works

1. You tell us about the site: what it does, rough traffic, which features you need.
2. We quote a monthly fee.
3. First month paid up front. Subsequent months by direct debit or card.
4. We provision, migrate or build, point DNS, issue SSL, test end-to-end.
5. Go-live window agreed with you. Simple setups land inside one working week; migrations with significant existing content can take longer, quoted per case.

## Enquire

Email hello@elmspark.com with:

- What the site does (service, shop, practice, and so on).

- Whether you are migrating from another host, another platform, or starting fresh.

We reply within one working day with a quote.
