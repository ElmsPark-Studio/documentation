Two decisions pick your page.

1. **How many sites?** Solo (one or two), SME (one busy site with real traffic), or Agency (a fleet of client sites).
2. **Who runs the box?** You (self-hosted, usually with Claude Code doing the bash work) or us (managed: ElmsPark Studio provisions, patches and monitors).

Find your match below and click through to the page written for you.

## Solo: one or two sites

- [Solo, managed](/hosting/solo-managed/). You run your business, we run the hosting. Best if SSH and DNS are not how you want to spend your time.
- [Solo, self-hosted](/hosting/solo-diy/). $12 per month Vultr plan plus a pasteable Claude Code prompt. About 30 minutes from empty box to live site.

## SME: one busy site, real traffic

- [SME, managed](/hosting/sme-managed/). One important site, zero hosting headaches. Backups, uptime, updates and support included.
- [SME, self-hosted](/hosting/sme-diy/). $24 per month Vultr plan. Right-sized for a single professional service or small shop.

## Agency: fleet of client sites

- [Agency, managed](/hosting/agency-managed/). White-label managed hosting for your clients, run on our infrastructure.
- [Agency, self-hosted](/hosting/agency-diy/). The $55 per month VX1 box, sized for 50 to 100 client sites on dedicated CPU.

## Still deciding?

If you know the business side and not the mechanics, pick managed. If you can read a command line, or you are happy to let Claude Code read it for you, pick self-hosted. PageMotor runs the same either way.
