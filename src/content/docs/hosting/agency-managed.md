You sell websites, not hosting. But your clients need somewhere reliable to run their PageMotor sites, and setting up a Vultr fleet is not how you want to spend your afternoon.

Managed agency hosting from ElmsPark Studio gives you a fleet to sell from, with the sysops work done for you.

## Who this is for

- Agencies, freelancers or consultancies who build and look after multiple PageMotor sites for clients.
- You have five to a hundred or more client sites, or you expect to within a year.
- You do not want to build and run a sysops function.
- You want to bill clients for hosting at a healthy margin and keep delivery simple.

## What you get

- **Shared dedicated VPS, or your own dedicated box.** A VX1 2 vCPU, 8 GB, 120 GB box at $55 per month comfortably runs 50 to 100 light-traffic PageMotor sites. For larger clients or stricter isolation needs, single-client boxes are an option.
- **Per-site provisioning.** New client sites spun up in minutes from an approved template.
- **Nightly backups per site.** Independent per-client restore, not one blob of everything.
- **SSL per site.** Let's Encrypt, fully automated.
- **Per-site uptime monitoring.** Alerts when any individual site goes down, not just when the box does.
- **Core and plugin maintenance.** PageMotor and EP Suite updates tested across a staging fleet and rolled out on a schedule.
- **White-labelled where it matters.** Your clients see your brand on the sites, in support replies, and on the invoices you send them. The ElmsPark Studio infrastructure layer stays invisible to them.

## What you bring

- Your clients and their domains.
- Your PageMotor licence or licences, if your offering includes paid EP Suite plugins.
- A single commercial relationship with ElmsPark Studio. We invoice you, you invoice your clients.

## Pricing shape

Monthly base fee for the VPS, plus a per-site maintenance fee. Tier-based discount on the per-site fee as your fleet grows. Exact numbers depend on traffic profile, backup retention, and support level. You get a written quote after the enquiry call, with all per-site and per-box costs itemised so you can set your client pricing with full visibility.

## How it works

1. You enquire with a rough shape: how many sites now, how many in twelve months, which countries your clients are mostly in.
2. We talk through whether a shared or dedicated VPS fits better.
3. We quote.
4. We provision the first box, migrate a pilot client, and work together through the handover and the first ten sites.
5. You move the rest across at whatever pace suits you.

## Enquire

Email hello@elmspark.com with:

- A short description of your agency or practice.
- Rough fleet size now, rough fleet size in twelve months.
- The countries your clients are mostly in (affects data centre choice).
- Whether you already host them somewhere and are looking to consolidate, or starting fresh.

We reply within one working day.
