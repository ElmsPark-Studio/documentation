One or two PageMotor sites, no hosting hassle. We provision the VPS, keep it patched, handle backups, and fix problems before you notice them.

## Who this is for

- You own one or two PageMotor sites.
- You want to focus on your work, not on SSH keys, Nginx vhosts, or SSL renewals.
- You would rather pay a monthly fee than learn Linux.

## What you get

- **Dedicated VPS.** Your sites run on their own Vultr box, not shared with strangers.
- **Backups every night.** One-click restore if anything goes wrong.
- **Free SSL.** Set up once, renews itself forever.
- **Monthly core and plugin maintenance.** PageMotor and EP Suite updates tested and applied on a scheduled monthly window.
- **Outbound email wired up.** SendGrid SMTP so contact form notifications, password resets and newsletter dispatch actually arrive.
- **Monitoring.** If the site goes down, we know before you do.
- **Support.** Same-day email support for anything site-related.

## What you bring

- A domain, registered anywhere (Namecheap, Cloudflare, GoDaddy, Porkbun, any of them).
- Your PageMotor licence if you use paid EP Suite plugins.
- A credit card for the monthly bill.

## How it works

1. You get in touch with a short summary of what you need.
2. We quote a monthly fee based on plan size and number of sites.
3. First month paid up front.
4. We provision the VPS, install PageMotor, migrate existing content if any, point DNS and issue SSL.
5. Sites go live once DNS has propagated. Usually within a week of enquiry.
